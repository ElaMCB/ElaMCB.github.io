About Me

I am an AI-First Quality Engineer dedicated to solving the fundamental challenge of reliability in generative AI and LLM applications. My expertise lies in building validation frameworks that ensure AI systems are safe, accurate, and trustworthy before they reach production.

Specializing in LLM Testing, AI Model Validation, Prompt Engineering, and AI Safety protocols, I create systematic approaches to test the unpredictable nature of AI systems. My frameworks catch hallucinations, prevent prompt injections, and measure model drift before they impact users.

My mission is to be the quality assurance layer that AI companies need but often overlook - ensuring their innovative systems don't embarrass them, lose customers, or create liability issues.
