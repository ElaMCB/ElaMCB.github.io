An Ethical AI Risk Register Template You Can Maintain in 15 Minutes

A lightweight register design for teams that need traceability without adding heavy governance overhead.

What to track (and what to ignore)

Track risks that can change a release decision: harmful outputs, unfair outcomes, privacy leakage, unsafe tool actions, and misuse patterns. Ignore broad philosophical notes that cannot be actioned in sprint planning.

For each risk, record: trigger, impact, current controls, residual risk, owner, and next review date.

Cadence that does not collapse

Update the register in three moments only: before release, after incidents, and when model/data context changes. This keeps it alive without creating a weekly ceremony people skip.

Pin one KPI to the register health: percent of risks with an owner and next review date.

Use incidents as input, not blame

When something goes wrong, add a new row and one control update. Do not rewrite the whole framework.

This creates an institutional memory that survives team turnover and helps future approvals move faster.
