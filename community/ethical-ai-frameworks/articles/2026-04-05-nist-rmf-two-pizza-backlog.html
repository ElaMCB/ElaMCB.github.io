From NIST AI RMF to a Two-Pizza Team Backlog

Map Govern, Map, Measure, and Manage to concrete stories so a small team does not drown in abstraction.

Govern without a bureaucracy

Pick one accountable owner for AI risk in the product line, one deputy, and a single escalation path. Document that in your wiki and link it from your repo README. That satisfies the spirit of Govern faster than a twelve-person council that never meets.

Map and Measure as release gates

Turn Map into an inventory: data sources, model versions, downstream users, and known failure modes. Turn Measure into a short list of metrics or qualitative checks that run before major releases—aligned with whatever you already track for quality and safety.

Manage is your backlog: mitigations, accepted risks with expiry dates, and incidents that feed the next Map update. Revisit the map when the model, data, or regulation changes—not only at annual compliance time.
